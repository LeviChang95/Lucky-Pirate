Lucky Pirate

Welcome to lucky pirate a pirate themed gambling game. Find the treasure chests filled with gold ( ) but avoid the skulls ( ) or loose your fortune!

How to Play

Tap the pirate ship and spend 100 gold to begin. Within the treasure chests 250 Gold pieces can be found, tap a chest to open it winning the gold inside. However two skulls are hidden amoungst the gold, if you tap a skull you lose the game and all the money you may have won. You can stop playing at any time by tapping the X button, keeping any gold you may have earned.
